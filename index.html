About Unstoppable News

Unstoppable News allows anyone to post an article to the Sonic blockchain. The only cost is the gas fee which is often less than $0.001 US. No company or government or individual, including myself (the creator), can censor or remove any of your articles. Even if the site goes down, all articles will remain accessible to the public via any Sonic blockchain explorer. Your articles are immutable and cannot be edited after published. Your article will be attributed to your wallet and readers will be able to donate a specified amount of SONIC to you after reading.

90% of these funds will be sent to the author and 10% will be sent to maintain the website.
